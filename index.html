About CyberOctober

CyberOctober is part of the National Cybersecurity Awareness Month, designed to increase awareness, spark interest in the field, and empower students and faculty with real-world cyber defense fundamentals. We host interactive workshops, games, and industry talks to help you learn and network!

Event Schedule & Interactive Sessions

  • Oct 10 (11:30 AM – 1:00 PM): Cybersecurity Certifications and Careers
  • Oct 17 (11:30 AM – 1:00 PM): Fundamentals of Cybersecurity (XDR, IAM, Zero Trust, MITRE ATT&CK)
  • Oct 24 (11:30 AM – 1:00 PM): Governance, Privacy, Audit, and Law
  • Oct 30 (2:00–5:00 PM): UNT Information Security Symposium - Panel 1: Hiring the Cyber Workforce, Student Papers
  • Oct 31 (9:00 AM–5:00 PM): AI for Cybersecurity, Campus to Cyber Command (Panels 2–3)
Featured activities: Games, Q&A, CTI Bingo, Threat Actor Case Studies, Interactive Polls.
